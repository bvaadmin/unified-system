Policies & Agreement

Crouse Memorial Chapel Wedding Policies

  • The chapel seats approximately 80 guests
  • No alcohol is permitted on chapel premises
  • Photography is allowed during the ceremony with restrictions
  • Rice, birdseed, and confetti are not permitted
  • Decorations must be approved by the Director of Worship
  • The chapel is available from May through October
  • Cancellations must be made at least 30 days in advance for refund
