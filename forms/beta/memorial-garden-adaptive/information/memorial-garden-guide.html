About the Memorial Garden

The Bay View Memorial Garden provides a peaceful, beautiful location for the placement of cremated remains within our historic community. Located on the grounds of Bay View Association, the garden offers a permanent memorial space for members and their families.

History

Established to serve the Bay View community, the Memorial Garden has become a cherished part of our 150-year tradition. It provides a sacred space where families can remember and honor their loved ones within the community they treasured.

Location

The Memorial Garden is situated on the Bay View campus, offering a serene setting with beautiful landscaping and peaceful surroundings. The exact location and visiting hours are available through the Bay View office.

Who is Eligible

Members

All Bay View Association members in good standing are eligible for placement in the Memorial Garden. This includes:

  • Current leaseholding members
  • Associate members
  • Life members
  • Immediate family members of the above

Non-Members

Non-members may be eligible for placement with:

  • Sponsorship from a current Bay View member
  • Demonstrated connection to the Bay View community
  • Approval from the Memorial Garden Committee

Types of Placement

Immediate Placement

For those who have recently passed away and whose families wish to arrange placement as soon as possible. This typically includes:

  • Coordination with funeral services
  • Scheduling of placement ceremony
  • Installation of memorial marker

Future Placement (Prepayment)

Plan ahead and secure your place in the Memorial Garden. Benefits include:

  • Lock in current pricing
  • Peace of mind for you and your family
  • Ensure your wishes are documented
  • Reduce burden on family during difficult times

Costs and Fees

Category Fee Notes
Bay View Members $400 Current members in good standing
Non-Members $1,000 Requires member sponsorship
Prepayment Same as above Locks in current rate

Payment Methods: Check, money order, or cash. Make checks payable to "Bay View Association" with "Memorial Garden" in the memo line.

Refund Policy: Prepayments are fully refundable if plans change.

The Process

What Information You'll Need

Required Information:

  • Full name of person for memorial placement
  • Date of passing (for immediate placement)
  • Contact person's name, phone, and address
  • Bay View membership status (person or sponsor)

Helpful Additional Information:

  • Date and place of birth
  • Parents' names (for genealogy records)
  • Bay View history and memories
  • Special service requests

Step 1: Application

Complete the Memorial Garden application form. You can provide just the required information to start.

Step 2: Review

The Memorial Garden Committee reviews all applications, typically within 3-5 business days.

Step 3: Approval

Upon approval, you'll receive confirmation and payment instructions.

Step 4: Payment

Submit payment to secure your placement or prepayment.

Step 5: Coordination

For immediate placement, coordinate service details. For prepayment, receive your documentation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I prepay for multiple family members?

Yes, you can prepay for multiple family members. Each person requires a separate application and fee.

What if I prepaid but can no longer use the placement?

Prepayments are fully refundable. You may also transfer the prepayment to another eligible family member with committee approval.

Are there any ongoing maintenance fees?

No, the one-time fee covers perpetual care and maintenance of the Memorial Garden.

Can we hold a service in the Memorial Garden?

Yes, memorial services can be held in the garden. Coordinate with the Bay View office for scheduling.

What type of memorial markers are allowed?

The Memorial Garden maintains uniform markers for all placements to ensure a cohesive, peaceful appearance.
